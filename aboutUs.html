OUR MISSION here at Smiles for Nursing Homes is to provide our beloved elderly citizens with the most hospitable care possible. Smiles for Nursing Homes is a place where young, ambitious college students or individuals can sign up to volunteer at neighboring nursing homes in and around the Norman area. Providing a loving, affectionate, and devoted community is what we strive to do everyday at Smiles for Nursing Homes!

Who are we?

Smiles for Nursing Homes is a non-profit organization designed by four first-year students at The University of Oklahoma with hopes of providing opportunities for young individuals to make a difference in their community!
